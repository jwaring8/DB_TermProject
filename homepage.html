FAQ:


Will I become rich trading stocks?

Maybe or maybe not, that's part of the fun. Trading stocks is like gambling but with more educated decisions and information available. You can do it for fun or as a way to diversify your portfolio there's many reasons why people buy and trade stocks.

How do I pick stocks?

Ask 100 people this question and you'll likely get a lot of different answers. Here's what we suggest, pick stocks from companies in areas that intreset you. For example, if you're into technology, specifically Apple, invest in Apple. It makes researching companies more interesting and allows you to stay ontop of what's going on in the industry and market.

What is a dividend?

A dividend is a portion of a company's profits to its stockholders, those who own stocks for that comapny. A dividend is often decided by the board of directors for that comapny. Simple put, the company gives money to its stockholders as a way of keeping investors.

What is diversification?

Diversification is the process in which one diversifies their portfolio to mitigate risk. In the case of economic events that cause a stock's price to decrease, a person does not lose a large amount of money. By diversifying one's investments into different companies, indices, and markets, the overall risk to losing large amounts of money is decreased.

What external sources do you reccommned?

There are many good sources to increase one's knowledge of the stock market and how to invest correctly. Countless websites and books offer a plethora of information for those just starting out to invest and those who have been investing professionally.

Some good books to read to get started with investing are:

  • The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham
  • A Random Walk Down Wall Street by Burton G. Malkiel
  • The Big Short by Michael Lewis
  • One Up On Wall Street by Peter Lynch

Some good websites to visit to get started and to learn more about investing are:
